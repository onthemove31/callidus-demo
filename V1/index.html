About Us

Established in London in 2003, Callidus is a multi-jurisdictional compliance consultancy. We operate out of London, Dubai, and Chennai.

Our expertise in compliance and regulatory affairs extends to all insurance and reinsurance businesses, including:

  • Lloyd's Managing Agents
  • Syndicates
  • Insurers
  • MGAs
  • Brokers
  • Other Intermediaries


We offer a range of tailored, efficient, and cost-effective solutions to a broad range of businesses.

We take great pride in our unwavering commitment to accuracy and consistent delivery of services. Our focused services are backed by extensive expertise and experience in the insurance industry.
